About
this
blog.

Cosmic Ink is a student astronomy blog created as part of the MDS 2 — AI for Natural Sciences course at St. Xavier's College, Kolkata. It explores the universe through the lens of a first-year, second-semester BSc Chemistry student who finds that the boundary between chemistry and astrophysics is thinner than you might think.

Every element heavier than hydrogen was made in stars. Every planet formed from the same cloud of dust and gas. This blog exists to tell those stories — accessibly, accurately, and with genuine curiosity. Conceived and written with a little help from AI.
